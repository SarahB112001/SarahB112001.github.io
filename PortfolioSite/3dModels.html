3d environment: "What Remains"



A 3d environment created using multiple assets and the base assets in Mixamo. The structure of the enviroment is based off the remains of sunken ships, with a visible skeleton as well as multiple chunks of damaged metal poking out of the ground. The meaning of the work's title shows that even after a catastrophe, life still goes on, even if damaged or changed.
